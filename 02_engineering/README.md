# Engineering Department | قسم الهندسة

## Department Overview | نظرة عامة

**English**: The engineering department designs RAS systems from initial site assessment through detailed technical specifications and construction drawings.

**العربية**: يصمم قسم الهندسة أنظمة RAS من تقييم الموقع الأولي حتى المواصفات الفنية التفصيلية ورسومات البناء.

---

## Workflow | سير العمل

### 1. Site Assessment | تقييم الموقع
- **Files**: `/site_assessment/`
- Site survey templates
- Environmental assessment
- Utilities availability checklist
- Access and logistics evaluation

### 2. System Design | تصميم النظام
- **Files**: `/system_design/`
- RAS system layouts
- Equipment selection criteria
- Capacity calculations
- Flow diagrams

### 3. Technical Specifications | المواصفات الفنية
- **Files**: `/technical_specifications/`
- Equipment specifications
- Material specifications
- Performance requirements
- Quality standards

### 4. Drawings & Documentation | الرسومات والوثائق
- **Files**: `/drawings/`
- CAD drawings
- P&ID diagrams
- Electrical schematics
- As-built documentation

### 5. Engineering Calculations | الحسابات الهندسية
- **Files**: `/calculations/`
- Hydraulic calculations
- Biofilter sizing
- Oxygen demand
- Pump selection
- Heat load calculations

---

## Key Tools | الأدوات الرئيسية

- AutoCAD / Revit
- Excel calculation templates
- Equipment selection software
- Hydraulic modeling tools

---

## Handoff to Construction | التسليم للبناء

**Deliverables**:
- ✅ Complete technical drawings
- ✅ Equipment list with specifications
- ✅ Bill of quantities (BOQ)
- ✅ Installation guidelines
- ✅ Testing protocols

**Training Duration**: 3-6 months
