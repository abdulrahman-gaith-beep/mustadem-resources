# Biology Department | قسم الأحياء

## Department Overview | نظرة عامة

**English**: Responsible for all biological aspects of fish farming including species selection, health management, nutrition, and biosecurity.

**العربية**: مسؤول عن جميع الجوانب البيولوجية لتربية الأسماك بما في ذلك اختيار الأنواع وإدارة الصحة والتغذية والأمن الحيوي.

---

## Workflow | سير العمل

### 1. Species Selection | اختيار الأنواع
- **Files**: `/species_selection/`
- Species profiles (tilapia, salmon, shrimp, etc.)
- Market demand analysis
- Growth rate comparisons
- Environmental requirements
- Economic viability

### 2. Health Management | إدارة الصحة
- **Files**: `/health_management/`
- Disease identification guides
- Treatment protocols
- Vaccination schedules
- Quarantine procedures
- Health monitoring protocols

### 3. Nutrition & Feeding | التغذية والإطعام
- **Files**: `/nutrition/`
- Feed specifications by species
- Feeding schedules
- Feed conversion ratio (FCR) tracking
- Nutritional requirements
- Feed suppliers database

### 4. Stocking Protocols | بروتوكولات التخزين
- **Files**: `/stocking_protocols/`
- Fingerling specifications
- Acclimation procedures
- Stocking density calculations
- Growth projections
- Harvest planning

### 5. Biosecurity | الأمن الحيوي
- **Files**: `/biosecurity/`
- Biosecurity protocols
- Visitor management
- Equipment disinfection
- Water treatment requirements
- Staff hygiene procedures

---

## Key Responsibilities | المسؤوليات الرئيسية

- Monitor fish health daily
- Adjust feeding programs
- Maintain water quality standards
- Conduct regular sampling
- Manage disease outbreaks
- Optimize growth rates
- Minimize mortality

---

## Coordination With | التنسيق مع

- **Production**: Daily operations and feeding
- **Engineering**: System design for species requirements
- **Finance**: Cost optimization and ROI
- **Farm Management**: Production planning

**Training Duration**: 6-12 months (requires aquaculture background)
