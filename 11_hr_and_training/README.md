# HR & Training Department | قسم الموارد البشرية والتدريب

## Department Overview | نظرة عامة

**English**: Manages recruitment, onboarding, training, performance management, and organizational development.

**العربية**: يدير التوظيف والتأهيل والتدريب وإدارة الأداء والتطوير التنظيمي.

---

## Workflow | سير العمل

### 1. Onboarding | التأهيل
- **Files**: `/onboarding/`
- New hire welcome package
- Company orientation materials
- Department-specific onboarding plans
- First 30/60/90 day checklists
- Paperwork and documentation

### 2. Job Descriptions | الوصف الوظيفي
- **Files**: `/job_descriptions/`
- All positions across departments:
  - Sales & Marketing
  - Engineering
  - Construction
  - Biology
  - Production Operations
  - Finance
  - Investment & SPV
  - Farm Management
  - Technology
  - Equipment & Suppliers
- Responsibilities and requirements
- Career progression paths

### 3. Training Programs | برامج التدريب
- **Files**: `/training_programs/`
- **Technical Training**:
  - RAS fundamentals
  - Water quality management
  - Equipment operation
  - Biosecurity protocols
  - Safety procedures
- **Soft Skills Training**:
  - Communication
  - Leadership
  - Problem-solving
  - Customer service
- **Certification Programs**
- Training calendars and schedules

### 4. Performance Management | إدارة الأداء
- **Files**: `/performance/`
- Performance review templates
- KPI frameworks by role
- Goal setting procedures
- Feedback mechanisms
- Performance improvement plans
- Reward and recognition

### 5. Organizational Structure | الهيكل التنظيمي
- **Files**: `/org_structure/`
- Company org charts
- Department structures
- Reporting relationships
- Team compositions
- Staffing plans

## Employee Journey | رحلة الموظف

### From Application to Leadership

1. **Recruitment** → Application, interviews, offer
2. **Onboarding** → First week orientation
3. **Training** → Department-specific training (1-12 months)
4. **Integration** → Working independently
5. **Development** → Skill enhancement, cross-training
6. **Advancement** → Promotions, leadership roles
