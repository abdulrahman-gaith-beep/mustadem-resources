# Investment & SPV Department | قسم الاستثمار والشركات ذات الأغراض الخاصة

## Department Overview | نظرة عامة

**English**: Manages investment relationships, SPV structures, fundraising, and investor relations.

**العربية**: يدير العلاقات الاستثمارية وهياكل الشركات ذات الأغراض الخاصة وجمع الأموال وعلاقات المستثمرين.

---

## Workflow | سير العمل

### 1. Pitch Materials | مواد العرض
- **Files**: `/pitch_materials/`
- Investor pitch decks
- Executive summaries
- Company overview
- Market opportunity
- Team credentials

### 2. Financial Projections | التوقعات المالية
- **Files**: `/financial_projections/`
- Detailed financial models
- Revenue projections
- Return analysis (IRR, NPV)
- Exit scenarios
- Risk analysis

### 3. Due Diligence | العناية الواجبة
- **Files**: `/due_diligence/`
- Data room checklist
- Technical due diligence
- Financial due diligence
- Legal due diligence
- Q&A documents

### 4. Legal Structure | الهيكل القانوني
- **Files**: `/legal_structure/`
- SPV formation documents
- Shareholder agreements
- Term sheets
- Investment agreements
- Governance structure

### 5. Investor Relations | علاقات المستثمرين
- **Files**: `/investor_relations/`
- Investor update templates
- Performance reports
- Board materials
- Communication schedules

---

## Investment Process | عملية الاستثمار

1. **Sourcing** - Identify potential investors
2. **Pitching** - Present opportunity
3. **Due Diligence** - Provide documentation
4. **Negotiation** - Terms and structure
5. **Closing** - Legal execution
6. **Post-Investment** - Ongoing reporting

---

## Types of Investors | أنواع المستثمرين

- **Strategic Partners**: Industry players
- **Family Offices**: High net worth individuals
- **Private Equity**: Professional investors
- **Government Funds**: Public sector
- **Development Banks**: ADF, IFC, etc.

---

## Key Documents | المستندات الرئيسية

- Investment memorandum
- Financial model (3-statement)
- Market analysis
- Technical feasibility study
- Legal opinions
- Insurance documentation

**Training Duration**: 3-6 months (finance/investment background required)
