# Construction Department | قسم البناء

## Department Overview | نظرة عامة

**English**: Manages the physical construction and installation of RAS facilities from groundbreaking to commissioning.

**العربية**: يدير البناء المادي وتركيب منشآت RAS من بداية البناء حتى التشغيل.

---

## Workflow | سير العمل

### 1. Project Management | إدارة المشاريع
- **Files**: `/project_management/`
- Project schedules (Primavera/MS Project)
- Resource allocation
- Budget tracking
- Progress reporting

### 2. Site Supervision | الإشراف على الموقع
- **Files**: `/site_supervision/`
- Daily site reports
- Contractor coordination
- Material receiving logs
- Installation supervision

### 3. Quality Control | مراقبة الجودة
- **Files**: `/quality_control/`
- Inspection checklists
- Testing procedures
- Non-conformance reports
- Quality assurance documentation

### 4. Safety | السلامة
- **Files**: `/safety/`
- Safety protocols
- Risk assessments
- Incident reporting
- PPE requirements
- Emergency procedures

### 5. Commissioning | التشغيل
- **Files**: `/commissioning/`
- System testing procedures
- Performance verification
- Water quality testing
- Equipment calibration
- Training for operations team

---

## Key Phases | المراحل الرئيسية

1. **Mobilization** (Week 1-2)
2. **Civil Works** (Month 1-3)
3. **Equipment Installation** (Month 3-5)
4. **System Integration** (Month 5-6)
5. **Testing & Commissioning** (Month 6-7)
6. **Handover** (Month 7)

---

## Handoff to Operations | التسليم للعمليات

**Deliverables**:
- ✅ As-built drawings
- ✅ Equipment manuals
- ✅ Test reports
- ✅ Training completion certificates
- ✅ Maintenance schedules
- ✅ Warranty documentation

**Training Duration**: 2-4 months
