# Operations Handover Checklist

## Overview

This checklist ensures a complete and effective handover from the project team to operations at the conclusion of commissioning. A thorough handover is essential for successful ongoing operations.

---

## Pre-Handover Requirements

Before initiating handover, confirm the following are complete:

### Construction and Commissioning Completion
- [ ] Construction substantially complete
- [ ] All punchlist items closed or have accepted plan
- [ ] All systems commissioned and tested
- [ ] Biofilter established and stable
- [ ] Trial stocking successful
- [ ] Performance tests passed

### Documentation Ready
- [ ] All required documents compiled
- [ ] Documents organized and accessible
- [ ] Electronic and physical copies prepared
- [ ] Document index created

### Operations Team Ready
- [ ] Operations staff hired
- [ ] Key personnel in position (Farm Manager, Supervisors)
- [ ] Training program completed
- [ ] Competency verified

### Regulatory Readiness
- [ ] Aquaculture license obtained
- [ ] SFDA registration complete
- [ ] BAP certification in progress or obtained
- [ ] All permits current

## Sign-Off Procedures

### Handover Meeting

**Agenda:**
1. Review of handover checklist completion
2. Discussion of any outstanding items
3. Confirmation of responsibilities post-handover
4. Questions and concerns
5. Formal handover signing

**Attendees Required:**
- Project Manager
- Construction Manager
- Farm Manager
- Operations Supervisor
- Client Representative
