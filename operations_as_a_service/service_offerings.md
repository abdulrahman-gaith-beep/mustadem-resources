# Service Offerings

## Overview

Mustadem provides comprehensive operations services for RAS (Recirculating Aquaculture System) facilities across Saudi Arabia and the GCC region. Our service packages are designed to meet the diverse needs of aquaculture operations, from remote monitoring to full-service farm management.

---

## Pricing Models

### Model 1: Per-Ton Pricing

**Best For:** Standard and Premium packages with defined production volumes

**Structure:**
- Fee calculated per metric ton of fish produced
- Adjusts automatically with production scale
- Incentivizes production efficiency

**Indicative Rates (SAR/ton produced):**

| Package | Low Volume (<100t/yr) | Medium (100-500t/yr) | High (>500t/yr) |
|---------|----------------------|---------------------|-----------------|
| Standard Operations | 800-1,200 | 600-900 | 400-600 |
| Premium Full-Service | 2,500-3,500 | 1,800-2,500 | 1,200-1,800 |

*Note: Actual rates depend on species, facility complexity, location, and contract terms.*

**Payment Terms:**
- Monthly billing based on previous month production
- Quarterly true-up against actual harvest
- Annual adjustment based on audit

### Model 3: Revenue Sharing

**Best For:** Start-ups, clients preferring variable costs aligned with performance

**Structure:**
- Base fee plus percentage of gross revenue
- Aligns service provider incentive with client success
- Shared risk and reward

**Indicative Structure:**

| Package | Base Fee (SAR/month) | Revenue Share (%) |
|---------|---------------------|-------------------|
| Standard Operations | 15,000-30,000 | 5-8% |
| Premium Full-Service | 30,000-60,000 | 10-15% |

**Revenue Calculation:**
- Based on actual fish sales (invoiced revenue)
- Calculated monthly, paid following month
- Subject to minimum annual guarantee

**Advantages:**
- Lower initial cash outlay
- Incentivizes revenue maximization
- Aligns interests with client

**Disadvantages:**
- Higher total cost if revenues high
- Complexity in revenue attribution
- Potential disputes on revenue recognition

### Case Study 2: Production Optimization

### Case Study 3: Emergency Response Saves Stock

**Client Profile:**
- Medium-scale barramundi operation
- Emergency Response retainer client
- Weekend incident

**Incident:**
- Friday evening: Main circulation pump failure
- Backup pump failed to auto-start
- DO levels dropping rapidly

**Response Timeline:**
| Time | Action |
|------|--------|
| 18:45 | Alarm triggered, client notified |
| 18:52 | Client called emergency hotline |
| 18:55 | Remote diagnosis initiated |
| 19:10 | Technician dispatched (45 km away) |
| 19:15 | Remote guidance: manual aeration activated |
| 19:20 | DO stabilized at 4.2 mg/L |
| 20:00 | Technician arrived on-site |
| 20:30 | Backup pump manually activated |
| 22:00 | Main pump motor replaced |
| 22:30 | Full system restored |

**Outcome:**
- Fish mortality: 0.1% (minimal stress losses)
- Estimated value saved: SAR 350,000+
- Root cause: Relay failure in auto-start circuit
- Prevention: Relay replacement, monthly auto-start test added to SOP

**Client Testimonial:**
> "Without Mustadem's emergency support, we could have lost our entire stock. Their rapid response and technical expertise saved our operation—and our investment. The retainer pays for itself many times over."
