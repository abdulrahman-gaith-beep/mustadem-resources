## Design Guidelines

### Color Palette
**Primary:**
- Deep Blue (#003366)
- Teal (#00A9A5)
- White (#FFFFFF)

**Accent:**
- Gold/Copper (#D4AF37) for highlights

**Background:**
- Clean white with light blue or teal accents
- Use color blocks to separate sections

### Typography
**Headlines**: Bold sans-serif, 18-24pt  
**Subheadings**: Bold sans-serif, 14-16pt  
**Body Text**: Clean sans-serif, 10-12pt  
**Contact Info**: 10-11pt

### Images
- 1-2 high-quality images maximum (front side)
- Professional RAS facility photos
- Clean, modern, high-tech aesthetic
- **NEVER use low-resolution or stretched images**

### Icons
Use simple, professional icons for:
- Water drops (efficiency)
- Shield (biosecurity)
- Chart (data/ROI)
- Building (infrastructure)
- Fish (obvious)
