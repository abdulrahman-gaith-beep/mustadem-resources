# Farm Management Department | قسم إدارة المزرعة

## Department Overview | نظرة عامة

**English**: Oversees overall farm performance, production planning, staff management, and strategic decision-making.

**العربية**: يشرف على الأداء العام للمزرعة وتخطيط الإنتاج وإدارة الموظفين واتخاذ القرارات الاستراتيجية.

---

## Workflow | سير العمل

### 1. Production Planning | تخطيط الإنتاج
- **Files**: `/production_planning/`
- Annual production plans
- Stocking schedules
- Harvest calendars
- Capacity utilization
- Market demand forecasting

### 2. Staff Management | إدارة الموظفين
- **Files**: `/staff_management/`
- Work schedules
- Performance evaluations
- Training coordination
- Team communication
- Conflict resolution

### 3. Inventory Management | إدارة المخزون
- **Files**: `/inventory/`
- Feed inventory tracking
- Equipment spare parts
- Chemicals and supplies
- Minimum stock levels
- Purchasing procedures

### 4. Maintenance Management | إدارة الصيانة
- **Files**: `/maintenance/`
- Preventive maintenance schedules
- Equipment service logs
- Repair procedures
- Maintenance budget
- Vendor coordination

### 5. KPI Tracking | تتبع مؤشرات الأداء
- **Files**: `/kpi_tracking/`
- Production KPIs
- Financial KPIs
- Operational KPIs
- Quality KPIs
- Dashboard templates

---

## Key Responsibilities | المسؤوليات الرئيسية

**Strategic**:
- Set production targets
- Optimize profitability
- Plan capacity expansion
- Manage stakeholder relationships

**Operational**:
- Coordinate all departments
- Ensure quality standards
- Manage resources efficiently
- Resolve operational issues

**Financial**:
- Budget management
- Cost control
- Revenue optimization
- ROI improvement

---

## Reports To | يقدم تقاريره إلى

- Company Management
- Investors/Board
- Client (if contract farming)

**Coordinates With**:
- All departments

**Training Duration**: 6-12 months (requires extensive experience)
