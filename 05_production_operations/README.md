# Production Operations Department | قسم عمليات الإنتاج

## Department Overview | نظرة عامة

**English**: Manages day-to-day farm operations including feeding, water quality management, and harvest.

**العربية**: يدير العمليات اليومية للمزرعة بما في ذلك الإطعام وإدارة جودة المياه والحصاد.

---

## Daily Operations | العمليات اليومية

### 1. Morning Routine (6:00 AM - 10:00 AM)
- System checks and equipment inspection
- Water quality testing (DO, pH, temperature)
- Mortality check and removal
- First feeding
- Record observations

### 2. Midday Operations (10:00 AM - 2:00 PM)
- Second feeding
- Water quality adjustments
- Tank cleaning
- Equipment maintenance
- Data logging

### 3. Afternoon Operations (2:00 PM - 6:00 PM)
- Third feeding
- Final water quality checks
- Prepare for night operations
- Update production logs
- Report any issues

## Key Folders | المجلدات الرئيسية

### `/daily_operations/`
- Daily checklist templates
- Operational SOPs
- Shift handover logs
- Equipment operation guides

### `/feeding_schedules/`
- Feeding calendars by tank/species
- Feed quantity calculations
- Feeding behavior observations
- FCR tracking

### `/water_quality/`
- Testing schedules
- Parameter target ranges
- Adjustment procedures
- Quality control logs

### `/harvest/`
- Harvest planning
- Size grading procedures
- Handling protocols
- Logistics coordination
- Sales documentation

### `/sop/`
- Standard Operating Procedures for all activities
- Emergency response procedures
- Safety protocols
- Quality standards

## Performance Metrics | مؤشرات الأداء

- Daily feed consumption
- FCR (Feed Conversion Ratio)
- Mortality rate
- Growth rate
- Water quality compliance
- Equipment uptime

---

## Handoff Points | نقاط التسليم

**From Construction**: Fully commissioned system
**To Farm Management**: Production data and performance reports
**To Biology**: Health observations and concerns

**Training Duration**: 1-3 months
