# Investment Pitch Outline

## Overview

This document provides a comprehensive template for developing investor presentations for aquaculture projects. An effective pitch communicates the opportunity, demonstrates competence, and inspires confidence in the investment.

---

## Pitch Deck Structure

### Standard Pitch Deck Sections

| Slide | Topic | Duration | Purpose |
|-------|-------|----------|---------|
| 1 | Title | 30 sec | Brand and identify |
| 2 | The Problem | 1 min | Create urgency |
| 3 | The Opportunity | 1 min | Show potential |
| 4 | Our Solution | 2 min | Present approach |
| 5 | Market Analysis | 2 min | Validate opportunity |
| 6 | Business Model | 2 min | Explain economics |
| 7 | Competitive Advantage | 1 min | Differentiate |
| 8 | Financial Projections | 2 min | Show returns |
| 9 | Team | 1 min | Build confidence |
| 10 | The Ask | 1 min | Clear call to action |
| 11 | Appendix | Reference | Supporting detail |

**Total Presentation Time:** 15-20 minutes + Q&A

## Pitch Best Practices

### Preparation

- [ ] Know your numbers cold (revenue, costs, returns)
- [ ] Prepare for tough questions
- [ ] Research your audience
- [ ] Practice, practice, practice
- [ ] Have backup slides ready
- [ ] Bring printed leave-behinds

### Delivery

- [ ] Start with a hook (compelling fact or question)
- [ ] Tell a story, not just facts
- [ ] Use visuals effectively (less text, more images)
- [ ] Make eye contact
- [ ] Manage time (leave time for Q&A)
- [ ] Be confident but not arrogant
- [ ] Address risks proactively

### Common Investor Questions

**Prepare answers for:**
1. Why aquaculture? Why now?
2. What is your unfair advantage?
3. Why should we invest in you vs. others?
4. What happens if [X assumption] doesn't hold?
5. How will you use our money?
6. What is the exit strategy?
7. What are the biggest risks?
8. What traction do you have?
9. Who else is investing?
10. What are your terms?

### Follow-Up

- [ ] Send thank you within 24 hours
- [ ] Provide requested information promptly
- [ ] Set clear next steps
- [ ] Follow up as agreed
- [ ] Keep investors updated on progress
