# Onboarding Guide

## Overview

This comprehensive onboarding guide ensures new employees at Mustadem and our partner aquaculture facilities are properly integrated into the organization. The program covers orientation, safety training, technical education, and ongoing professional development.

| Day | SOP | Activity |

| Mon | SOP-001: Water Quality Testing | Read, observe, practice |

| Thu | SOP-005: Health Assessment | Read, observe, practice |
| Fri | SOP-006: Equipment Maintenance | Read, observe, practice |

**SOP Training Methodology:**

1. **Read** the SOP document thoroughly
2. **Observe** experienced staff executing the procedure
3. **Assist** with the procedure under supervision
4. **Execute** the procedure independently (observed)
5. **Sign off** when competency demonstrated

## Ongoing Development

### Mentorship Program

**Program Structure:**

**Mentor Assignment:**
- Each new employee assigned a mentor for first 6 months
- Mentor is experienced staff member in same or similar role
- Regular check-ins scheduled

**Mentorship Activities:**

| Week | Frequency | Activity |
|------|-----------|----------|
| 1-4 | Daily | Check-in with mentor |
| 5-12 | Weekly | 30-min 1:1 meeting |
| 13-26 | Bi-weekly | 1-hour development discussion |

**Mentor Responsibilities:**
- Answer day-to-day questions
- Provide guidance on best practices
- Share knowledge and experience
- Help navigate organizational culture
- Flag concerns to management

**Mentee Responsibilities:**
- Come prepared with questions
- Be open to feedback
- Proactively seek learning opportunities
- Communicate challenges early

### Career Progression

**Career Paths:**

**Technical Track:**
```
Worker → Aquaculture Technician → Senior Technician → Lead Technician → Technical Specialist
```

**Management Track:**
```
Technician → Shift Supervisor → Assistant Farm Manager → Farm Manager → Operations Director
```

**Specialist Track:**
```
Technician → Fish Health Specialist → Veterinary Coordinator
Technician → Water Quality Specialist → Environmental Manager
Technician → Equipment Specialist → Maintenance Manager
```

**Advancement Criteria:**

| Level | Typical Tenure | Requirements |
|-------|---------------|--------------|
| Entry Level | 0-1 year | Complete onboarding, basic competency |
| Technician | 1-3 years | All SOPs mastered, good performance |
| Senior Technician | 3-5 years | Leadership skills, training others |
| Supervisor | 5+ years | Management training, demonstrated leadership |
| Manager | 7+ years | Full operational capability, business acumen |

**Promotion Considerations:**
- Consistent strong performance reviews
- Demonstrated competency at current level
- Completion of relevant training/certifications
- Leadership potential (for management track)
- Business need for role at higher level
