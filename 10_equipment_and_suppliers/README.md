# Equipment & Suppliers Department | قسم المعدات والموردين

## Department Overview | نظرة عامة

**English**: Manages equipment specifications, supplier relationships, procurement, and equipment maintenance.

**العربية**: يدير مواصفات المعدات وعلاقات الموردين والمشتريات وصيانة المعدات.

---

## Equipment Categories | فئات المعدات

### 1. Tanks & Rearing Systems | الخزانات وأنظمة التربية
- **Files**: `/tanks/`
- **Types**:
  - Circular tanks
  - Rectangular tanks
  - Raceway systems
  - Dual-drain systems
- **Materials**: Fiberglass, concrete, HDPE, stainless steel
- **Specifications**: Size, volume, wall thickness
- **Suppliers Database**: Global manufacturers

### 2. Filtration Systems | أنظمة الترشيح
- **Files**: `/filters/`
- **Mechanical Filtration**:
  - Drum filters
  - Screen filters
  - Settling tanks
  - Protein skimmers
- **Biological Filtration**:
  - Moving bed biofilters (MBBR)
  - Fixed bed biofilters
  - Fluidized bed filters
  - Trickling filters
- **Specifications & Suppliers**

### 3. Pumps & Circulation | المضخات والدوران
- **Files**: `/pumps/`
- **Types**:
  - Centrifugal pumps
  - Submersible pumps
  - Airlift pumps
  - Dosing pumps
- **Selection Criteria**: Flow rate, head, efficiency
- **Major Brands**: Grundfos, Pentair, Xylem

### 4. Aeration & Oxygenation | التهوية والأكسجة
- **Files**: `/aeration/`
- **Equipment**:
  - Blowers and air pumps
  - Air diffusers
  - Oxygen cones
  - LOX systems (liquid oxygen)
  - Oxygen generators (PSA/VSA)
- **Design Calculations**
- **Energy Efficiency**

### 5. Biofilters & Media | المرشحات الحيوية والوسائط
- **Files**: `/biofilters/`
- **Media Types**:
  - Plastic carriers (K1, K3, K5)
  - Ceramic rings
  - Bio-balls
  - Lava rock
- **Surface Area Calculations**
- **Nitrification Capacity**
- **Suppliers & Pricing**

### 6. Water Treatment | معالجة المياه
- UV sterilizers
- Ozone generators
- Dechlorinators
- Water heaters/chillers
- pH adjustment systems

### 7. Feeding Systems | أنظمة التغذية
- Automatic feeders
- Demand feeders
- Manual feeding equipment
- Feed storage silos

### 8. Monitoring & Control | المراقبة والتحكم
- See Technology Systems department

- Lead times
- Quality certifications

- After-sales support

## Procurement Process | عملية المشتريات

1. **Specification** - Define requirements
2. **RFQ** - Request for quotation
3. **Evaluation** - Technical and commercial
4. **Negotiation** - Terms and pricing
5. **Purchase Order** - Official order
6. **Tracking** - Delivery monitoring
7. **Inspection** - Quality check
8. **Installation** - Commissioning
9. **Documentation** - Manuals and warranties

---

## Equipment Selection Criteria | معايير اختيار المعدات

- **Technical Suitability**: Meets specifications
- **Reliability**: Proven track record
- **Cost**: CAPEX + OPEX
- **Availability**: Spare parts and service
- **Warranty**: Coverage and terms
- **Energy Efficiency**: Operating costs
- **Scalability**: Future expansion
